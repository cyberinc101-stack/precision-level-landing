Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Precision Level ("the app") is developed and published by HappyAppsForYou. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Precision Level runs entirely offline. The app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Sensor readings (accelerometer/gyroscope for roll and pitch) and calibration values are processed locally on your device only and never leave it.

Camera Permission

The AR Camera Level feature requests camera access solely to display a live overlay on your screen. Camera frames are not recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Advertising

The app may display ads served through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
